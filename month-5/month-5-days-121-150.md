## Tuần 17 (Days 121–127): Self-Introduction & STAR Method

### Day 121: Perfect Self-Introduction for Interviews

**Framework (2 phút):**

**Opening → Current → Past → Skills → Why Here**

> "Thank you for having me. My name is [tên]. I'm a [vị trí] with [X] years of experience in [lĩnh vực].
>
> Currently, I'm working at [công ty], where I'm responsible for [responsibilities]. Some of my key projects include [dự án 1] and [dự án 2].
>
> Before this role, I worked at [công ty cũ], where I [kinh nghiệm cụ thể].
>
> My technical skills include [tech stack]. I'm particularly strong in [strength].
>
> I'm excited about this opportunity at [công ty] because [lý do cụ thể]. I believe my experience in [lĩnh vực] aligns well with what you're looking for."

**Bài tập:** Viết, ghi âm, và luyện cho đến khi tự nhiên. MỤC TIÊU: 2 phút, không vấp, không nghe như đọc thuộc.

---

### Day 122: STAR Method Deep Dive

**S**ituation → **T**ask → **A**ction → **R**esult

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- S+T: 20% thời gian (bối cảnh ngắn gọn)
- A: 60% thời gian (hành động cụ thể CỦA BẠN)
- R: 20% thời gian (kết quả CÓ SỐ LIỆU)

**Mẫu hoàn chỉnh:**
> **S:** "At my previous company, our API response time had increased to over 3 seconds, causing user complaints."
>
> **T:** "As the senior developer, I was tasked with identifying the bottleneck and reducing response time to under 500 milliseconds."
>
> **A:** "First, I set up monitoring using Datadog to identify slow endpoints. I discovered that one database query was doing a full table scan. I added proper indexing and implemented a caching layer using Redis. I also introduced connection pooling for the database."
>
> **R:** "As a result, the average response time dropped from 3 seconds to 200 milliseconds — a 93% improvement. User complaints went down by 85%, and the system could handle 3x more concurrent users."

**Bài tập:** Viết 3 STAR stories.

### Day 123: STAR Story #1 — Problem Solving

**Câu hỏi trigger:** "Tell me about a time you solved a difficult technical problem."

**Checklist cho story:**
- [ ] Situation: Bối cảnh rõ ràng (công ty, dự án, vấn đề)
- [ ] Task: Vai trò CỦA BẠN trong vấn đề
- [ ] Action: ≥ 3 hành động CỤ THỂ bạn đã làm
- [ ] Result: Kết quả CÓ SỐ LIỆU (%, thời gian, tiền...)

**Bài tập:** Viết story → luyện nói 2 phút → ghi âm → sửa → luyện lại.

---

### Day 124: STAR Story #2 — Teamwork

**Câu hỏi:** "Tell me about a time you worked effectively as part of a team."

**Tips for teamwork stories:**
- Nhấn mạnh COLLABORATION, không phải chỉ mình bạn
- "I collaborated with the frontend team to..."
- "We held weekly syncs to align on..."
- "I took the initiative to set up..."
- Nhưng vẫn rõ ràng contributon CỦA BẠN

---

### Day 125: STAR Story #3 — Leadership / Initiative

**Câu hỏi:** "Tell me about a time you showed leadership."

**Tips:**
- Không cần là manager để show leadership
- "I noticed that... so I proposed..."
- "I volunteered to lead the migration because..."
- "I mentored two junior developers on..."

---

### Day 126: STAR Story #4 — Failure / Learning

**Câu hỏi:** "Tell me about a time you failed. What did you learn?"

**Framework cho failure stories:**
1. **Acknowledge the failure** honestly
2. **Explain what went wrong** (without blaming others)
3. **What you learned** and **what you changed**
4. **How it helped you** going forward

**Mẫu:**
> "Early in my career, I deployed a database migration without proper testing. It caused 30 minutes of downtime in production. I learned the importance of having a rollback plan and always testing migrations in staging first. After that, I implemented a deployment checklist that the whole team now uses. We haven't had a migration-related incident since."
